Privacy Policy for Score Keeper

Last Updated: December 17, 2025

Introduction

Score Keeper ("we," "our," or "the app") is a local-first score tracking application for board games and card games. This Privacy Policy explains how information is collected, used, and shared when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

Data Stored Locally on Your Device

All game data and scores you create in Score Keeper are stored locally on your device only. This includes:

  • Player names and scores
  • Game history and undo states
  • App preferences and settings
  • Orientation mode preferences
  • Audio playback settings

We do not have access to this data. It never leaves your device.

Information Collected by Third-Party Services

Google Firebase Analytics

We use Google Firebase Analytics to understand how users interact with our app and to improve the user experience. Firebase Analytics may collect:

  • Device information (device model, operating system version)
  • App usage data (screens viewed, features used, session duration)
  • General location information (country/region based on IP address)
  • App crashes and performance data
  • Device identifiers (Firebase Installation ID, Advertising ID)

Important: Firebase Analytics does not collect player names, scores, or any game content you create.

For more information about how Google uses data:

  • Firebase Privacy and Security
  • Google Privacy Policy

Google AdMob (Advertising)

We use Google AdMob to display advertisements in the app. AdMob may collect and use the following information:

  • Advertising identifiers (e.g., Google Advertising ID)
  • IP address
  • Device information (device type, operating system)
  • General location information (country/region)
  • Ad interaction data

For more information:

  • Google Privacy Policy
  • How Google uses information from sites or apps that use our services

You can opt out of personalized advertising by adjusting your device's advertising settings:

  • iOS: Settings > Privacy > Advertising > Limit Ad Tracking
  • Android: Settings > Google > Ads > Opt out of Ads Personalization

Sentry (Error Tracking)

We use Sentry to monitor app performance and track errors to improve app stability. Sentry may collect:

  • Device information (model, OS version)
  • App version information
  • Error logs and stack traces
  • Session data

This information is used solely to identify and fix bugs and does not include any of your personal game data.

For more information: Sentry Privacy Policy

Data Usage

How We Use Information

  • Local Data: Your game scores and player data are used only by the app on your device to provide score tracking functionality. This data is never transmitted to our servers or any third parties.
  • Analytics Data: Used to understand app usage patterns, identify popular features, and improve the user experience.
  • Error Data: Error information collected by Sentry is used to diagnose and fix technical issues.
  • Advertising Data: Collected by AdMob to serve relevant advertisements.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to third parties, except as described below:

  • Firebase Analytics: Usage data is processed by Google Firebase as described in their privacy policy.
  • AdMob: Advertising data is shared with Google as described in their privacy policy.
  • Sentry: Error and diagnostic data is processed by Sentry for crash reporting purposes.

Your local game data (player names, scores, game history) is never shared with anyone as it never leaves your device.

Data Security

Your game data is stored locally on your device using industry-standard secure storage mechanisms. We cannot access this data, and it is protected by your device's built-in security features.

Data Retention

  • Local Data: Stored on your device until you delete the app or clear app data.
  • Analytics Data: Retained by Google Firebase according to their retention policies (typically 14 months, configurable).
  • Error Logs: Retained by Sentry according to their retention policies (typically 90 days).
  • Advertising Data: Retained by Google AdMob according to their policies.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Delete all local data by uninstalling the app or clearing app data
  • Opt out of personalized advertising through your device settings
  • Opt out of Firebase Analytics by disabling analytics in your device settings
  • Request information about what data has been collected (contact us at the email below)

Children's Privacy

Our app does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The app is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children.

Audio Features

The app includes optional background music features. All audio playback is handled locally on your device. No data about your audio preferences or usage is collected or transmitted.

Screen Wake Lock

The app may keep your screen active during gameplay to prevent the device from sleeping while tracking scores. This is a local device feature and does not involve any data collection.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Data Deletion

Since all game data is stored locally on your device, you can delete all data by:

  1. Uninstalling the app, or
  2. Clearing the app's data through your device settings

This will permanently remove all your game scores and player information.

For data collected by third-party services (analytics, advertising), please refer to their respective privacy policies for data deletion procedures.
