Privacy Policy for Score Keeper

Last Updated: January 12, 2026

Daybreak Labs LLC ("we," "us," or "our") operates the Score Keeper mobile application. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

Information We Collect

Local Data (Stored on Your Device Only)

Player names, scores, game history, templates, and app settings are stored only on your device. This data never leaves your device and is deleted when you uninstall the app.

Analytics

We use Firebase Analytics to collect anonymous usage data (feature usage, device type, session duration). This data is not linked to your identity and helps us improve the app.

Crash Reports

We use Sentry to collect crash reports (error logs, device info, stack traces) to identify and fix bugs. This data is not linked to your identity.

Advertising

We display banner ads via Google AdMob.

For EEA, UK, and Switzerland users: When you first start a game, you'll see a consent dialog where you can choose:

  • Personalized ads - Google may collect your advertising ID, IP address, and ad interactions to show relevant ads
  • Non-personalized ads - Ads based only on app content and general location, with no personal data used

You can change this anytime in Settings → Ad Preferences.

For users outside EEA/UK/Switzerland: Personalized ads are shown by default. You can opt out in Settings → Ad Preferences.

iOS 14.5+: If you allow personalized ads, you'll also see Apple's App Tracking Transparency prompt.

Third-Party Services

  • Firebase Analytics - Usage analytics (Privacy Policy)
  • Google AdMob - Advertisements (Privacy Policy)
  • Sentry - Crash reporting (Privacy Policy)

We never sell, rent, or trade your personal information.

Data Retention

  • Local data: Until you delete the app
  • Analytics: 2-14 months (Firebase)
  • Crash reports: 90 days (Sentry)
  • Ad consent: Stored locally until reset

Your Rights

Change ad preferences: Go to Settings → Ad Preferences → Ad Personalization

Delete your data: Uninstall the app to delete all local data. For analytics/crash data deletion, contact us.

EEA/UK users (GDPR): You have the right to access, correct, delete, restrict, object to, or port your data. Contact us to exercise these rights.

California residents (CCPA): You have the right to know what data we collect, request deletion, and opt out of data sales (we don't sell data).

Children's Privacy

We don't knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 (or 16 in the EEA). All analytics data is anonymous. No account creation is required.

International Data Transfers

Data may be processed in the United States where our service providers operate. For EEA/UK users, transfers comply with GDPR via Standard Contractual Clauses.

Security

We use industry-standard security measures including secure device storage and HTTPS encryption. However, no method is 100% secure.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Significant changes will be announced in-app. Check the "Last Updated" date above for the latest version.
