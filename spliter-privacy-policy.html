Privacy Policy for Splitter

Last Updated: December 17, 2025

Introduction

Splitter ("we," "our," or "the app") is a local-first expense splitting application. This Privacy Policy explains how information is collected, used, and shared when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

Data Stored Locally on Your Device

All expense data, group information, and settlements you create in Splitter are stored locally on your device only. This includes:

  • Expense details (amounts, descriptions, dates)
  • Group member names
  • Settlement calculations
  • App preferences and settings

We do not have access to this data. It never leaves your device.

Information Collected by Third-Party Services

Google AdMob (Advertising)

We use Google AdMob to display advertisements in the app. AdMob may collect and use the following information:

  • Advertising identifiers (e.g., Google Advertising ID)
  • IP address
  • Device information (device type, operating system)
  • General location information (country/region)
  • Ad interaction data

For more information about how Google uses data, please review:

  • Google Privacy Policy
  • How Google uses information from sites or apps that use our services

You can opt out of personalized advertising by adjusting your device's advertising settings:

  • iOS: Settings > Privacy > Advertising > Limit Ad Tracking
  • Android: Settings > Google > Ads > Opt out of Ads Personalization

Sentry (Error Tracking)

We use Sentry to monitor app performance and track errors to improve app stability. Sentry may collect:

  • Device information (model, OS version)
  • App version information
  • Error logs and stack traces
  • Session data

This information is used solely to identify and fix bugs and does not include any of your personal expense data.

For more information: Sentry Privacy Policy

Data Usage

How We Use Information

  • Local Data: Your expense data is used only by the app on your device to provide expense splitting functionality. This data is never transmitted to our servers or any third parties.
  • Error Data: Error information collected by Sentry is used to diagnose and fix technical issues.
  • Advertising Data: Collected by AdMob to serve relevant advertisements.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to third parties, except as described below:

  • AdMob: Advertising data is shared with Google as described in their privacy policy.
  • Sentry: Error and diagnostic data is processed by Sentry for crash reporting purposes.

Your local expense data is never shared with anyone as it never leaves your device.

Data Security

Your expense data is stored locally on your device using industry-standard secure storage mechanisms. We cannot access this data, and it is protected by your device's built-in security features.

Data Retention

  • Local Data: Stored on your device until you delete the app or clear app data.
  • Error Logs: Retained by Sentry according to their retention policies (typically 90 days).
  • Advertising Data: Retained by Google AdMob according to their policies.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Delete all local data by uninstalling the app or clearing app data
  • Opt out of personalized advertising through your device settings
  • Request information about what error data has been collected (contact us at the email below)

Children's Privacy

Our app does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The app is not directed to children under 13.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Data Deletion

Since all expense data is stored locally on your device, you can delete all data by:

  1. Uninstalling the app, or
  2. Clearing the app's data through your device settings

This will permanently remove all your expense information.
