Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need an account or internet connection to use Splitter?

No account, no sign-in, and no internet required. Splitter works offline and keeps your groups and expenses on your device, so it's fast, private, and always available on trips, flights, and low-signal areas.

How do I split a bill unevenly or by exact amounts?

Choose a custom split and assign exact amounts per person. Splitter automatically recalculates balances and simplifies who owes who, even when one person paid for multiple items.

How can I share what everyone owes if they don't have the app?

Use Share on a group or settlement to send a clean summary by iMessage, email, or any sharing app. Your friends do not need Splitter installed to see the final amounts.

I changed phones. Can I keep my groups and history?

Yes. Create a backup from Settings before switching devices, then restore it on your new phone. If you uninstall without a backup, local data cannot be recovered.

How do ads work, and can I control ad personalization?

Splitter is free to download and use, and ads help keep it that way. You can control ad personalization anytime in Settings → Ad Preferences, and full data details are in the Privacy Policy.
